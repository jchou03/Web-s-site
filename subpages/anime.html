Anime

Boku no Hero Academia

A shonen anime based in a universe where humans are born with special abilities called quirks. A boy named Midoriya Izuku is born quirkless and becomes a target for bullying. However, Midoriya continues to strive for his dream to become a hero that smiles and laughs in the face of danger to in still no fear in the population and be the face of the heroes.

Seishun Buta Yarou wa Bunny Girl Senpai no Yume wo Minai

A boy named Sakuta Azusagawa meets a girl, named Mai Sakurajima, in a bunny costume at a library where no one seems to notice her. The cause for no one noticing her is because of a phenomenon called Puberty Syndrome. Sakuta then meets other kids who suffer from the same phenomenon and attempts to fix them.

Sword Art Online: Alicization

Taking place after the events of Sword Art Online: Gun Gale Online, Kirito takes a part time job as a subject of a full-dive system where he loses all memories of diving in and coming out. In this full-dive universe he has two friends named Alice and Eugeo. Coming out of the full-dive Kirito is sent into a coma and wakes up in the full-dive universe and must find a way to get back to the real world.

Kaguya-sama wa Kokurasetai

An anime about how two members of the student council, Shirogane and Kaguya, attempt to get the other to confess their love to them to avoid the love dominance that follows after due to pride. Through multiple rounds of overthinking and using their braincells to produce an optimal outcome for them. Cut into three short stories for each episode.

Tsuki ga Kirei

A very quiet anime that uses silence as a means of communication between two introverted lovers named Akane and Kotorou. Through 12 episodes they begin to develop a relationship and break out of their awkward shell.

Shigatsu wa Kimi no Uso

Kousei, a once piano prodidgy, meets a girl named Kaori, who helps him through his trauma of playing the piano with help from friends Tsubaki and Watari, Kousei finds himself back on the stage with the piano. Kousei must find himself in the music to reach his friends, family, and most importantly Kaori.

Made in Abyss

There is a hole that is unexplored in the world, and every is interested it and a city springs up around it to explore it. Riko is a 12 year old girl and she is very interested in the hole and she wants to go deep into it to find her mother. She feels this unyielding desire to go into it. She gets a robot boy named Reg to go explore the hole and they do it together.

Konosuba

Post death Kazuma, a shut in NEET, he is brought into heaven and meets a god named Aqua. She laughs at him and allows him to take one thing with him on to his next life, Kazuma takes her, and together they go off in this new world through hilarious trials and tribulations while suffering at the hands of themselves because they're all bad people.

Plastic Memories

Taking place in the near future, where androids look and behave like humans and are basically indistinguishable from humans, a boy named Tsukasa applies for a job at an android retrieval service and is assigned to a veteran android named Isla. Despite flaws in how she goes about retrieving androids, they continue to do their jobs and Tsukasa realizes that the job is much more emotionally taxing to do, especially his last one with Isla.
